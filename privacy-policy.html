Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

At Omkar Services Group, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity Data: Name, date of birth, PAN card, Aadhaar (for KYC compliance)
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, address
  • Financial Data: Bank account details, income information, investment history
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide loan facilitation services (connecting you with banks/NBFCs)
  • To verify your identity and conduct KYC checks
  • To communicate with you about your applications
  • To send you educational content and updates (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Data Sharing

We may share your information with partner banks (HDFC, Axis, Kotak, etc.) for loan processing, and with regulatory authorities when required by law. We do NOT sell your personal data.
